Greenville Roleplay Operation™

Public Services Handbook

This Handbook Is Only Intended For Greenville Roleplay Operation. If you are found using this for another server you will be blacklisted.

Public Services Regulations:

1.1. Common Sense

  • Please use common sense. Not everything is crystal clear in rules. Verify punishments are fair.

1.1.1 Discord ToS

  • Follow Discord Terms of Service: Abide by the platform's guidelines and rules.

1.1.2 Roblox ToS

  • Follow Roblox Terms of Service: Abide by the platform’s guidelines and rules.

1.2. Leaking Confidential Information & Channels

  • Do not leak channels, documents, or group chats involving GVRO P/S assets or private conversations. Violations result in immediate termination & unappealable blacklist.

1.3. Professionalism & Respect

  • Maintain professionalism and respect. Avoid violence, cursing, or negative behavior. Violations result in moderation.

1.4. Abuse of Authority

  • Abusing kick, ban, warn, mute, etc. will result in immediate action. GVRO P/S does not condone abuse of authority.

1.5. Drama Within & Outside of GVRO Public Services

  • Drama is prohibited. Open a support ticket for issues. Improper handling may result in bad reputation and moderation.

1.6. Moderation Enforcement

  • Department Command+ must have valid evidence when issuing infractions. Punishments without evidence may be revoked.

1.7. Traffic Response

  • Entering the wrong flow of traffic while on duty is strictly prohibited.

1.8. U-Turns

  • U-turns over concrete medians may damage vehicles. Use pullover lanes.

1.9. Reckless Driving

  • Reckless driving is strictly prohibited, including running stop signs or exceeding posted speed limits.

2.0. Responding Speeds

  • Maximum 105 MPH (168 KM/H) safely when responding to emergencies or pursuits.

2.2. Proper Etiquette

  • Use proper grammar, tone, and callouts while on patrol. Excessive normal talk is not allowed.

2.3. Realistic Avatars

  • Wear realistic avatars while on-shift. Tails, pets on shoulders, etc. are prohibited. Belts, hats, bodycams, and radios are allowed.

2.4. Civilian Suspension

  • Civilian suspended members cannot join sessions as public safety; department suspension applies if violated.

2.5. Terminations

  • After termination, wait 2 weeks to reapply or fastpass. Rejoining the same department may be restricted.

2.6. Allowed To Pull Over

  • Only civilians may be pulled over. Pulling over staff, host, or co-host is prohibited.

2.7. Unauthorized Chasing

  • Unauthorized chasing (Leakers, FRPers, etc.) is prohibited. Only High Speed Unit may chase.

2.8. ELS System

  • ELS may only be used for calls, stops, or on-scene. Abuse may result in strikes or suspension.

2.9. Department Slander

  • Treat all departments equally. Respect is mandatory.

3.0. Listening to Safety Relations Committee

  • Follow all Safety Relations Committee instructions. Do not disrespect, harm, or overpower them.

Public Services Policies:

4.0. Radio Traffic Only VC

  • Members must be in dispatch VCs for communication during patrols.

4.1. Promotions

  • Promotions are based on activity, quota completion, or necessity. Inactivity may result in warnings or demotion.

4.2. Resignations

  • Members may resign anytime. Exact rank may not be retained. Must wait 14+ days to reapply.

4.3. Leave of Absence

  • LOA: 7–28 days. Must wait 10+ days before requesting another. Abuse results in removal and write-up.

4.4. Quota Requirements

  • Trial PSM–PSM: 1 hour
  • Board of Managers: 45 min
  • Head of Safety Relations Manager: 30 min
  • Overseers: Exempt. Supervise/Attend.
  • Quota checked every 2 weeks. Log 30+ minutes per session on Sundays.

4.5. Chain of Command

4.6. Staff Vehicles Per Rank Category

Punishment Guide

4.8.1 Verbal Warning Offenses

  • Reckless Driving 1st Offense
  • Abusing ELS & Sirens
  • Responding to unrelated calls
  • Not actively in RTO
  • Forgetting to log shifts
  • Improper radio etiquette
  • Patrolling wrong / parking wrong
  • Going 4–9 MPH over FRP
  • Going over posted speed limit without valid reason
  • Not utilizing callsigns
  • Excessive normal talk in RTO
  • Pulling over staff or other P/S
  • Any repeated verbal warnings may result in strikes or suspension

4.8.2 Strike Offenses

  • Refusing to listen to Department Staff+
  • False ticket / punishment (LEO)
  • Repeated FRP
  • Going 105+ MPH when responding
  • Not following the PS Handbook or department rules
  • Ramming, desyncing, cutting off cars
  • Unrealistic RP
  • Interfering with staff scenes
  • Using wrong vehicles
  • Disrespect to civilians or P/S members
  • Giving extra time while logging shifts
  • Going 10+ MPH over FRP
  • Joining while civilian suspended
  • Failure to complete quota
  • Using P/S vehicle while off-duty
  • Impersonating higher rank
  • Spamming/Spam pinging
  • Using wrong callsign
  • Pulling over someone without reason
  • Chasing FRPer or leaker

4.8.3 Suspension Offenses

  • Mass FRP
  • Mass disrespect
  • Abusing powers
  • Reaching 3+ strikes
  • Patrolling while not trained
  • Not responding to calls
  • Farming hours (AFK)

4.8.4 Termination Offenses

  • Failure to meet quota multiple times
  • Inactivity
  • Unrealistic RP multiple times
  • Not following department regulations
  • Mass FRP (more than suspension)
  • Farming hours multiple times (AFK)
  • Anytime deemed necessary by staff

4.8.5 Blacklist Offenses

  • Impersonating a department without proper roles
  • Mass violation of department regulations or handbook punishments
