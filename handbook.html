Greenville Roleplay Operation™

Public Services Handbook

This Handbook Is Only Intended For Greenville Roleplay Operation. If you are found using this for another server you will be blacklisted.

Public Services Regulations:

1.1. Common Sense

• Please use common sense. Not everything is gonna be crystal clear and said in rules. Go over our regulations and verify the punishment deemed is fair.

1.1.1 Discord ToS

• Follow Discord Terms of Service: Abide by the platform's guidelines and rules.

1.1.2 Roblox ToS

• Follow Roblox Terms of Service: Abide by the platform’s guidelines and rules.

1.2. Leaking Confidential Information & Channels

• As a member in Greenville Roleplay Operation’s Department Relation’s Server, you are not allowed to leak any channels, documents, or group chats that involve GVRO P/S assets, information, or private conversations. Failure to respect our privacy will result in immediate termination & unappealable blacklist from P/S.

1.3. Professionalism & Respect

• You are required to be professional & respectful to everyone. Failure to do so will result in moderation against yourself. We ask that you are nice to everyone, patient, using non-violent words, cursing, or just overall having a bad attitude towards others.

1.4. Abuse of Authority

• Any act of abusing kick, ban, warn, mute, etc will result in immediate action towards yourself. We do not condone the act of AA at GVRO P/S, failure to acknowledge our regulations & policies will only result in you being dismissed from staffing.

1.5. Drama Within & Outside of GVRO Public Services

• We do not condone drama in any channel regardless of the issue. If you have a problem with a civilian or staff member open a ticket and have a High Ranking Official take care of it. Causing drama & improperly handling it will result in a bad reputation for the server & yourself. We ask that you just open a support ticket and have someone deal with it professionally & properly.

1.6. Moderation Enforcement

• All Safety Relations Committee/Department Command+ are required to have valid evidence when issuing infractions, mutes, bans, kicks, etc. Failing to utilize proof when moderating will result in consequences that can lead to termination. If no valid evidence is present, the punishment can be revoked.

1.7 Traffic Response

• Entering The Wrong Flow Of Traffic While On Duty Is Strictly Prohibited and Will Result In Moderation If You Do So.

1.8 U-Turns

• Conducting a u-turn Over a Concrete Median Would Realistically Cause Heavy Damage To Your Department Issued Vehicle, So Please Refrain From Doing This. Instead Conduct a u-turn Inside Of The Pullover Lanes In The Middle Of The Roadway.

1.9 Reckless Driving

• Any Form Of Reckless Driving While On-Duty Is Strictly Prohibited, This Includes Running Stop Signs, Going Above The Posted Speed Requirements, Etc.

2.0 Responding Speeds

• You Must Go A Maximum of 105 MPH (168 KM/H) Safely When Responding To An Emergency Callout, Engaging in Motor Vehicle Pursuits, Etc.

2.2 Proper Etiquette

• While on patrol you not only should be in RTO but you should be using proper grammar tone and use of words and callouts at all times. Use of excessive normal talk is not allowed.

2.3 Realistic Avatars

• You are required to wear realistic avatars at all times while on-shift as public safety. Tails, Pets on shoulders, Etc is not allowed. Realistic avatar addons are allowed, Belts, Department hats, Bodycams, Radios, Etc.

2.4 Civilian Suspension

• When you are Civilian Suspended you cannot be joining sessions as Public Safety. Doing so will result in Department Suspension.

2.5 Terminations

• Once terminated from a department you have to wait at least 2 weeks to apply or fastpass to a department. You will not be able to rejoin the department you were fired from for a certain period of time that the department command desires.

2.6 Allowed To Pull Over

• The people you are allowed to pull over are civilians. You are never allowed to pull over other public services or host/co-host, even if you are staff yourself. Staff are not in roleplay.

2.7 Unauthorized Chasing

• You are not allowed to chase Leakers, Mass FRPers, etc. This is the staff’s job. The only authorized people to chase is High Speed Unit.

2.8 ELS System

• Emergency Lighting Systems on ANY Public Safety vehicle are only allowed to be used when responding to calls, while in a Motor Vehicle Stop, or on scene. Abusing your lighting System or Sirens may result in Department Strikes or Suspension.

2.9 Department Slander

• Treat all departments as equal. You need to respect all departments! If not done so you will be warned.

3.0 Listening to Safety Relations Committee

• You are required to listen to anyone apart of the Safety Relations Committee, you should not disrespect, harm, try to overpower them, etc. They have all authority to strike, terminate, suspend, and blacklist you from a department.

Public Services Policies:

4.0. Radio Traffic Only VC

• You are required to be in • Dispatch Communications, 1 or • Dispatch Communications, 2 when patrolling as any department.

4.1. Promotions

• Promotions will be given out if you complete quota, are active, or if needed. Inactive members may be warned, demoted, or terminated.

4.2. Resignations

• Public Service Members are allowed to resign at any given time. You may come back but may not get the exact rank back depending on duration of resignation.

4.3. Leave of Absence

• Public Service Members are allowed to request a LOA at any given time, but you must follow the guidelines and not abuse it. - Longest Duration: 28 Days - Shortest Duration: 7 Days Must Wait 10+ Days To Request Another

4.4 Quota Requirements

- Trial PSM-PSM- 1 Hour - Board of Managers - 45 Minutes - Head of Safety Relations Manager - 30 Minutes - Overseers - Exemption From Quota. Must Supervise/Attend • Quota is done every 2 weeks. On Sunday you must log sessions of 30+ minutes.

4.8. Public Service Punishments

4.8.1 Verbal Warning Offenses
• Reckless Driving 1st Offense (Swerving, Running Stop Signs, Going Above The Posted Speed Requirements, Etc.) • Abusing ELS & Sirens • Responding to an Unrelated Call (i.e. Police Helping with a Fire) • Not Actively In RTO • Forgetting To Log Required Shift Forms + Patrol Channel • Not Using Proper Radio Etiquette and Codes inside of RTO • Patrolling Wrong, Parking In the Wrong Area • Going 4-9 MPH Over FRP • Going Over Posted Speed Limit Without Valid Reason • Not utilizing callsigns • Using an excessive amount of normal talk inside RTO • Pulling over staff or other public services Any Verbal Warnings That Continue Will Result in Immediate Action (Department Strikes or Suspension)
4.8.2 Strike Offenses
• Refusing to Listen to Department Staff+ • False Ticket / Punishment (LEO) • Repeated FRP • Going 105MPH+ When Responding • Not Following the PS Handbook • Not Following Department Rules • Ramming, Desyncing, Cutting Off Cars (Even when Responding) • Unrealistic RP (i.e. EMS Giving wrong treatment, LEO Writing Wrong Penal Code etc.) • Interfering With Staff Scenes • Using Wrong Vehicles • Disrespect to ANY Civilian or P/S Member • Giving Extra Time While Logging Shifts • Going 10+ Over FRP • Joining While Civilian Suspended • Failure To Complete Quota • Using a P/S Vehicle While Off-Duty • Impersonating a Higher Rank • Spamming/Spam Pinging in Department Channels • Using the Wrong Callsign • Pulling over someone without reason • Chasing a FRPer or Leaker
4.8.3 Suspension Offenses
• Mass FRP • Mass Disrespect • Abusing Powers • Reaching 3+ Strikes • Patrolling While Not Being Trained • Not Responding to Calls • Farming Hours (i.e. Going online but being AFK)
4.8.4 Termination Offenses
• Failure to Meet Quota Multiple Times • Inactivity • Unrealistic Roleplay Multiple Times (See Strike #8) • Not Following Department Regulations • Mass FRP (More than Suspension) • Farming Hours Multiple Times • Anytime YOU See Fit. They Signed That They Can be Terminated at Any Time for Any Reason
4.8.5 Blacklist Offenses
• Impersonating A Department Without Proper Roles • Mass Violation of Department Regulations or Punishments In Handbook
